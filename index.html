Welcome girls!

As you all know, motorsports is more than just the driver and their vehicle; there is a whole group of people like engineers, analysts, social media people, team managers, etc., behind the scenes. This site is trying to help women start careers in the motorsports industry.

Explore career paths, training resources, and inspiring stories from women already working in the field.

Things that will help you get started

Steps

  • Study STEM subjects like engineering or data analytics or maybe something related to media
  • Look for internships or volunteer opportunities with motorsport teams
  • Network with professionals through events and online communities, influencers can let you know a lot of times when interships and student placements start.

Role Models

  1. Hannah Schmitz: Engineer in Redbull Formula 1 Team and their Star Strategist
  2. Susie Wolff: Former F1 Test Driver, Team Executive and Managing Director of F1 Academy
  3. Nadia Padovani: Engineer and technical expert in motorsports
