Opportunities in Motorsports Careers

Career Paths

There are many roles in motorsports besides driving:

  • Race Engineers:design, test, and optimize vehicles
  • Data Analyst:analyze race and performance data
  • Social Media Manager:manage team communications and engagement
  • Team Manager:coordinate team operations and logistics

Nadia Padovani said: "It’s not easy being a woman in a predominantly male world. It’s very important to be credible in the eyes of others, and that’s what makes being the first woman to lead a MotoGP team a daily challenge." So girls believe in yourselves it may be a hard journey, but who doesnt love to see a mans face when you succeed.
